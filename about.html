About

Hey there! Thanks for stopping by, please feel free to make yourself at home. My name is Kelsey Kassis, I am an interdisciplinary artist whose focuses are in the mediums of illustration, design, and textiles.

My work's themes are heavily influenced by queer identity, identity dysmorphia, finding joy and silliness in the mundane, and my connection to 'craft' as its traditionally been defined as "women's work".

Some common traits in my work are non-realistic body proportions (anti realism for the win!), bright pastels, prints-prints-prints, and plants/flowers.

Pronouns: she/her
